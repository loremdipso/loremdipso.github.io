I've got a huge reading list and go through seasons where I want to listen to an audiobook. For that I absolutely love [Libby](https://libbyapp.com/). You just add all your library cards and it'll let you know if a particular book is available at any of those locations.

Great! But doing this process manually for every book on my list until I find one that's available _right now_ is tedius. So I threw together this project (which is to-date possibly the ugliest thing I've ever made). Without any authentication (I ❤️ open APIs) it'll query your libraries for you and collate the results.

You can even install it as a PWA 😎
