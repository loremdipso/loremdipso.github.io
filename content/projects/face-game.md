I wanted to play around with modern JavaScript facial recognition, which seems to be [face-api.js](https://justadudewhohacks.github.io/face-api.js/docs/index.html). It was striking to me just how easy it was to get going. From requesting a camera feed to asking the library for the user's most likely expression, the whole project was very smooth.
