## Intro

FLTK, pronounced "fulltick"[^1], is an extremely practical yet underrated contender in Rust's [GUI Wars](https://www.areweguiyet.com/). It's also ugly as sin. Let's talk about it.

### A brief history

Initially released in 1998, FLTK has become the Bing to GTK and QT's Google.

This gem of a project wasn't exactly hit with the ugly stick but it has expertly dodged any attempt at beautification.

[^1]: I know, it's a bit of a stretch.
